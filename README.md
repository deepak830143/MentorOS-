# 🚀 MentorOS

> **An AI-Powered Government Exam Preparation Platform**

MentorOS is an intelligent learning platform designed for aspirants preparing for Government examinations such as **UPSC, APPSC, SSC, RRB, Defence, Banking, ISRO, DRDO, BEL**, and other competitive exams.

Unlike traditional exam preparation apps, MentorOS combines **official government notifications**, **daily current affairs**, **AI-powered learning**, **digital library**, **study planner**, and **mental wellness tracking** into one unified platform.

---

# 🌟 Vision

Build India's most comprehensive AI-powered exam preparation ecosystem that saves students time, reduces stress, and improves learning outcomes.
